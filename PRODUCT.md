# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Primary: a driving instructor in Ireland. Uses the app on their phone immediately after or during a lesson to log lesson details, typically outside at the car. Needs to be in and out in under 30 seconds. Secondary: the same instructor at a desk, reviewing student progress, exporting data, and (future) uploading records to the RSA/NDLS portal via automated form submission.

## Product Purpose

Track EDT (Essential Driver Training) lesson progress for student drivers through Ireland's mandatory 12-lesson curriculum. Log completed lessons against the correct logbook and lesson type, monitor each student's progress toward the full EDT, and export data for compliance and reporting. Future: automated submission to third-party licensing authority systems.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, Clear, Professional. A well-made work tool: confident and efficient, gets out of the way. Not a toy, not a corporate system. The instructor should trust it immediately.

## Anti-references

- Bloated enterprise tools: no heavy sidebars, nested menus, grey tables that look like SAP or Oracle.
- Generic Bootstrap CRUD: no default blue-on-white with no visual identity, no "obviously a tutorial app" look.
- Consumer/playful: no bright gradients, bubbly rounded corners, or emoji-heavy UI. This is a professional tool.

## Design Principles

1. **Speed on mobile first.** Every primary action (log a lesson) must be reachable in under 3 taps on a phone. No unnecessary taps or scrolling.
2. **Information when needed.** Progress and history are visible but secondary to the primary action flow.
3. **Outdoor legibility.** High contrast, legible type, generous touch targets. The instructor may be in bright sunlight.
4. **Grow gracefully.** Current features are clean and obvious; the layout leaves room for upcoming export and automation features without cluttering the primary flow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Touch targets 44px+. Sufficient contrast for outdoor daylight use.
